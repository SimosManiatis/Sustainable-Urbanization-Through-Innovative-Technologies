Sustainable Urbanization - Sensor Data Analysis Report
====================================================

1. METHODS
----------
Data Preprocessing:
- Time Alignment: Timestamps parsed to UTC.
- Gap Detection: Days with data gaps > 2 hours were excluded per node.
- Resampling: Data resampled to 1-minute resolution (interpolated max 15 mins).
- Feature Engineering: Rolling volatility (std_5min) and temporal deltas (delta_15min).
- Aggregation: 15-minute non-overlapping bins. Bins with <80% coverage dropped.
- Normalization: Z-score standardization per measurement type.

Clustering:
- Scope: Global clustering (all rooms combined) to ensure comparable states.
- Algorithm: K-Means (K=4).
- Features: Normalized variability and mean value features.
- Labeling: Clusters labeled by centroid intensity (Inactive -> High).
